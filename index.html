Where's My Hero!?

Our user is a tech savvy sandwich aficionado. Someone who won’t be fooled by any questionable piece of meat sitting lacklusterly between a pair of tired old buns. No! Our user expects the greatest culinary experience offered. But, the question remains, where to get it? Where’s their club, their open face, their BLAT ... where’s their hero?

That's where we come in. We host reviews of sandwiches to help our users find the best usage of their food oriented face holes. We find their hero and we tell them right where to shove it!

A Little About Me

I'm a trained full stack developer with a passion for front end development!

I feel like I could be related to Buddy the Elf from the movie Elf. Sugar is at least two of my favorite food groups.

I've spent years developing video games in a production role and some of my favorite days were where I got to express myself creatively working alongside the design team. This creativity is what I bring with me everywhere I go!

An example of my fingerprints on a game

The original intention for a portion of the Spiderwick Chronicles was for an X shaped seesaw in a forest clearing. The first pitch from the team was for a puzzle where the player would need to place a rock on one end of the seesaw to gain access to another area, and place weights on a second of the 4 ends to gain access to the final area to bypass the puzzle.

I suggested, and created basic design documentation for a brief underground maze followed by a chase sequence featuring an underused boss in an underground cavern leaping across islands of rock jutting out above a chasm of impossible depth with the camera turning around to face the player keeping the pursuing monster in view reinforcing the urgency.

Here you can see the fantastic results of the level designers, music artist, environment artists, character modelers and animators.
