Teachers And Students Connection or TASC is a project I created with an extremely talented team of fellow students. We realized that some of the resources for tracking class assignments, submitted work, and feedback provided on that work was spread out across many different software systems. We wanted to go through the process of attempting to fix a very real problem by trying to consolidate these tracking systems into a single location and we also wanted to make sure we identified the needs of both the teaching staff and the students. I’m extremely proud of what we were able to accomplish in a week!
